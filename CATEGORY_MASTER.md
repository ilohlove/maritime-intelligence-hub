## Normalization Notes

- Keep categories stable before training prompts or scoring rules depend on them.
- Avoid adding near-duplicates unless the editorial difference is clear.
- Use one primary category per article in MVP.
- Add secondary categories later if needed.

## MVP Grouping

For briefs, categories may be grouped into:

- Safety and Regulation.
- Market and Shipping.
- Ports and Logistics.
- Technical and Seafarer.
- Vietnam and Regional Impact.
