# CONTENT_FORMAT_MASTER.md

## Purpose

Define output formats before building publishers.

## Morning Brief

Audience: fast operational awareness.

Sections:

- Top maritime alerts.
- Safety and regulation.
- Shipping and market.
- Ports, logistics, and Vietnam impact.
- Source links.

## Evening Brief

Audience: end-of-day summary.

Sections:

- What happened today.
- Why it matters.
- Vietnam/logistics impact.
- Watchlist for tomorrow.
- Source links.

## Weekly Brief

Audience: strategic review.

Sections:

- Top trends.
- Regulatory watch.
- Safety lessons.
- Market and container movement.
- Port/logistics developments.

## Item Format

Each brief item should include:

- headline;
- 2-4 sentence Vietnamese summary;
- category;
- importance score;
- source name;
- original URL.

## Publishing Rule

Generate Markdown and JSON first. Add Telegram, social, or website publishers after the core format is stable.
