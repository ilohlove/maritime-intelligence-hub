# LEGAL_COPYRIGHT_POLICY.md

## Purpose

Protect Maritime Intelligence Hub from republishing copyrighted material improperly.

## Allowed

- Store article metadata.
- Store article title.
- Store original URL.
- Store source name.
- Store publication date.
- Store a short excerpt when reasonable.
- Generate original AI summaries.
- Link back to the source.

## Not Allowed

- Republishing full original articles.
- Hiding or removing source attribution.
- Publishing long copied passages.
- Circumventing paywalls.
- Ignoring robots or explicit legal restrictions.

## Medium Or High Risk Sources

For sources marked `Medium` or `High` copyright risk:

- prefer metadata and URL only;
- keep excerpts short;
- require legal notes before automated publishing;
- keep AI summary clearly transformative and concise.

## Publishing Rule

Every published item must include source name and original URL.
