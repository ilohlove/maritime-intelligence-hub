# AUDIENCE_MASTER.md

## Purpose

Define reader groups used for relevance scoring and brief tailoring.

## Recommended Audiences

- `All`
- `Seafarer`
- `Officer`
- `Shipowner`
- `Engineer`
- `Logistics`
- `Port`
- `ImportExport`
- `Business`
- `Regulator`

## MVP Rule

Use one primary audience per source and one primary audience per brief item. Add multi-audience tagging only after the first pipeline is stable.

## Relevance Notes

- `All`: major maritime news, regulation, safety, market disruption.
- `Seafarer`: onboard safety, professional practice, casualty lessons, technical guidance.
- `Logistics`: container carriers, ports, customs, congestion, trade lanes.
- `Shipowner`: regulation, charter market, operating costs, compliance.
- `Business`: market, investment, policy, macro logistics.
