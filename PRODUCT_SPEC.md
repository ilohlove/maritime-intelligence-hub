# PRODUCT_SPEC.md

## Product Name

Maritime Intelligence Hub

## Product Goal

Become the fastest practical AI-assisted maritime news intelligence hub for Vietnamese maritime professionals.

## Primary Users

- Maritime officers and seafarers.
- Shipowners and ship managers.
- Port and terminal operators.
- Shipping, logistics, and import-export teams.
- Maritime educators, analysts, and industry contributors.

## MVP Outcome

Generate reliable Morning Brief and Evening Brief outputs from selected maritime news sources with clear source attribution, category classification, importance scoring, and Vietnamese summaries.

## MVP Includes

- Source master import.
- Source validation.
- RSS-first collection.
- Approved HTML fallback.
- Metadata and URL storage.
- Duplicate detection.
- Category classification.
- Importance scoring.
- AI summary generation.
- Markdown and JSON brief output.

## MVP Excludes

- Public web portal.
- Complex GUI.
- Mobile app.
- Payment.
- Login and permissions.
- AI chat.
- AIS tracking.
- Knowledge graph.
- Forecasting.

## Product Principle

Do the smallest reliable pipeline that can repeatedly produce useful maritime briefs before building broader publishing and interface layers.
